Game Log

    How To Play

    Welcome to Spacemageddon. A simple game, requring 1-2 players, where you must be able to get to the end of the game with the most points (How generic). you might be saying but that's not all, this game has many surprises of Chaos. It's best to remember that this is an unforgiving game and it is heavily luck based. Alright let's begin.

      How to Play

    1. Because this is an online board game, a lot of the rules are taken care of for you, however, it is good to know what everything means.
    2. Players each turn must roll the die. Depending on the number, they move the amount rolled. (Note: the color of the die changes based on whose turn it is).
    3. Upon reaching the split tile, you may choose a path to go down (marked with a sign). Note that if an event were to occur where you are sent back to a space before the split tile (let's say an event that sends you back to start ;) ), you will be able to choose again upon reaching it again.

      How to Win

    1. The goal of the game is to have the most Victory Points in the end. Victory points are received from landing on green tiles, chaos cards, or reaching the last tile first.
    2. It should be remembered that whoever reaches the last tile gets a bonus of 5 Victory Points. There is no guarantee this will be enough for you to win the game but it can turn the tables too.

      What does...mean?

      What does each tile do?

    1. Gray Airlock Tile:

      This is the starting tile that both players begin at.

    2. Green $ Tiles:

      Points (and gravity) makes the station go around! You want to land on these tiles as much as possible, they are your win condition!

    3. Red X Tiles:

      Also known as "Chaos Tiles", will make you draw a random chaos card which will do as it implies...cause chaos.

    4. Blue Bridge Airlock Tile:

      The first person to reach this tile will end the game with a bonus of 5 points. NOTE: As nice as the bonus is, this does not guarantee you winning the game

      What is this Chaos I keep hearing about?

    • Chaos is a modifier that affects the severity of Chaos cards. Chaos increases by 1 every 4 rounds. (A round ends once all players have had a turn).
    • The current chaos level can be seen to the left. The amount of red icons indicate the amount of chaos. (Note: The literal objects on the icons are purely cosmetic)
